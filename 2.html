Dutch:
Mijn naam is William van de Wiel, ik ben geboren in Gorinchem op 18 februari 2000 en ben nu dus 18 jaar oud. Op dit moment woon ik 12 jaar in Papendrecht. Vanaf jong af aan was ik druk bezig met technologie en heb hier altijd al interesse ingehad. English:

My name is William van de Wiel, I was born in Gorinchem, Holland at the 18th of Februari in the year 2000, this means I am now 18 years old. Currently I live in Papendrecht, South-Holland, I've been living here for about 12 years so far, not a bad place. Just as I could walk I was interested in technology, it's always kept me busy. Digital technology will keep on growing exponentially, the following years will be a rollercoaster ride in terms of technological and digital innovation.
